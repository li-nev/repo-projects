Let's list out what are some possible things to consider when traveling:

What's your Budget?

You don't want to find yourself being low on money mid-trip especially if you have decided to do a two week trip. Planning your budget is probably one of the more important things to consider with your trip.
Setting more money aside than your budget would probably be a nice thing to do for yourself. Of course, you can't always fully plan for how much things cost. Being able to have some flexibility in your budget is ideal.

Consider who you're traveling with.

Whether not it's family, partner, or friends. It's important to make sure everyone involved has communicated their expectations of the trip. Trying to figure out what everyone would like to accomplish while traveling is important to have no friction or any issues.

Important Documents

It's rather important to get all your papers in a row. Especially if you're planning on staying out of the country.
For instance; Passport - Having the identification for International travel.
International Driver's License - if you've decided that your main mode of transportation is going to be driving your own vehicle.
With the pandemic happening, some places are still pretty strict about their regulations. It might be important to have documentation of the shots you have so that you deal with the least amount of obstacles when traveling.

There are definitely more than just the above items to cover.
Like home care, animal care, and plant care. Making sure that your bank knows where it is you're headed. The list goes on and on.
