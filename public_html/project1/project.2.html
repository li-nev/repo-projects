Above and Beyond Group Therapy Weekender

First off I should start off with saying that I’m not a great story teller but I’m going to talk about my trip with my closest friends to a music Festival. We started our planning to go to the ABGT: Weekender, several months in advance, due to the fact that there were about 30 of us. It was important to make sure we tracked who was flying in and who was driving up to Washington State.
The location of the music festival was at the Gorge Amphitheatre so making sure we had everything we needed was important. Especially since we had to drive in together, so we could camp next to one another.
The day of, we had nine vehicles that caravanned into the festival, it was such an exciting time! All of the cars were packed to the brim with camping equipment, water, food, and festival gear.

When we got there the energy was incredible; everyone was stoked to begin with the music, getting to know our neighbors, and settling our camping spots. The most difficult aspect of being there was probably the heat, it was in the high 90’s and so hard to keep cool.

Friday Night

Gorge Website

The first night of music was amazing! They had this huge setup right above the Gorge; there was a Ferris Wheel, a bus that had a DJ turn table set up, and a ginormous tent that was set up off to the side for another group of artists to play. There were so many lights, different food venders, and so many amazing outfits!
The music played until about midnight with amazing artists. Majority of people didn’t sleep all night and ended up having talks about what brought them to the Weekender. It was a wonderful time hearing people connect and share their life experiences as well.

Friday Night Artists:

  • Naz
  • Rodg
  • Nourey
  • Bexxie
  • Chris Giuliano

Saturday Night

ABGT Website

The second night, we were finally let out into the view of the Gorge with a stage set up right in front. We got to listen the sets of Fatum, Oliver Smith, and Andrew Bayer during the afternoon and when we got to watch the sun set Mat Zo started with his set and we ended the night with a set of Above and Beyond and Genix. The night was phenomenal after that, stayed up playing games with one another. We celebrated my boyfriends birthday that evening and ended up watching the sun rise.

Saturday Night Artists:

  • Nourey
  • Bexxie
  • Amy Wiles
  • aname
  • Fatum
  • Oliver Smith
  • Andrew Bayer
  • Mat Zo
  • Above and Beyond
  • Genix

Sunday Night

The last day for us was basically learning how to survive the heat. We started to dunk people in water and we had a whole bunch of people line up to get soaked. There were a lot of people playing games to try to keep cool, a ton of people really just spent their time under shade structures melting away.
When it finally cooled off, people started to make moves to get to the venue for the finale. The set was another evening of absolutely amazing music and connecting with my close friends with dance.

Sunday Night Artists:

  • Lakou Mizik and Joseph Ray pres. Leave the Bones
  • OLAN
  • Qrion
  • Franky Wah
  • Marsh
  • James Grant and Jody Wisternoff
  • Eli and Fur
  • Ben Böhmer

The Weekender was definitely an event that helped me find some sort of peace and adventure. Listening to music and dancing with people I consider my family was the nicest way to spend a weekend. I won’t ever forget this experience and will be replaying the memories until the next event.
