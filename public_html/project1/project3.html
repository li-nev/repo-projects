Executive Summary

The website I will be covering for my Accessibility Evaluation Report will be Reddit. This is a website I use constantly for the cute dog photos, hilarious videos, different type of information on upcoming video games, different software and hardware discussions, and any type of entertainment/information, really.
Based on this evaluation, reddit does not meet WCAG 2.1, Conformance Level AA. Detailed review results are available in Section 6 below. Resources for follow-up study are listed in Section 7 below. Feedback on this evaluation is welcome.

Background about Evaluation

The evaluation of the web accessibility needs to have a combination of evaluation tools and manual evaluation by a reviewer who is experienced. The results in this report are based on the evaluation that the reviewer has conducted on: 10/1/2022. The website may have changed since then.
